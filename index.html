Abstract

I am a PhD student at Mila and University of Montreal, co-supervised by Liam Paull and Yoshua Bengio. I enjoy working on generalizable and scalable representation learning, generative models, and unsupervised learning. During my PhD, I have also spent time working with amazing researchers from Google Brain, Apple, and Preferred Networks. Previously, I obtained my MPhil's degree at HKUST and my Bachelor's degree at Zhejiang University.
